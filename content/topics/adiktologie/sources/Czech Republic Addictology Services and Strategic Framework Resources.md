Here is the comprehensive documentation and export of the sources, links, and data materials used to build the knowledge base regarding addiction treatment and related services in the Czech Republic.  
**1\. Government & Strategic Concept Documents**

* **Koncepce adiktologických služeb pro děti a dorost (Concept of Addictology Services for Children and Adolescents):** A strategic conceptual framework created by the Child and Adolescent Addictology Section (DaDA) of the Society for Addictive Diseases, outlining the hierarchy and standards of youth addiction services 1\.  
* **Koncepce sítě specializovaných adiktologických služeb (Concept of the Network of Specialized Addictology Services):** A foundational policy document developed by the Society for Addictive Diseases and the Clinic of Addictology (1. LF UK and VFN in Prague) defining the scope and availability of outpatient and residential care 2, 3\.  
* **Sčítání adiktologických služeb 2020 (Census of Addictology Services 2020):** A detailed statistical mapping published by the National Monitoring Centre for Drugs and Addiction (NMS), documenting the capacities, organizational structures, and funding of addictology programs nationwide 4, 5\.

**2\. Clinical Guidelines & Methodologies**

* **Doporučené klinické postupy v adiktologii (Recommended Clinical Guidelines in Addictology):** Comprehensive evidence-based clinical protocols developed by the Czech Addictology Institute (2022–2024) to guide screening, diagnosis, harm reduction, and interventions 6\.  
* **Metodika moderních metod sociální práce a síťování v adiktologických službách (Methodology of Modern Social Work Methods and Networking):** A practical guide developed by Společnost Podané ruce o.p.s. under the RAS project, detailing outreach work, case management, and peer-to-peer practices 7, 8\.  
* **Metodika práce s dětmi a dospívajícími v oblasti digitální závislosti (Methodology for Working with Digital Addiction in Youth):** A specialized guide by the Clinic of Addictology focusing on screening, diagnostic tools, and therapeutic interventions for internet and gaming disorders 9, 10\.

**3\. National Portals, Helplines & Databases**

* **Mapa pomoci (Map of Help) – www.drogy-info.cz:** A frequently updated database operated by the National Monitoring Centre for Drugs and Addiction, allowing users to search for treatment, prevention, and harm reduction facilities across all regions 11, 12\.  
* **Národní linka pro odvykání (National Quitline) – www.chciodvykat.cz:** A free, toll-free helpline (800 350 000\) providing counseling for smoking, alcohol, drugs, gambling, and digital addictions 13-15.  
* **Šance Dětem – sancedetem.cz:** An informational portal (run by the Sirius Foundation) providing resources on family therapy, counseling, and child-rearing challenges 16, 17\.

**4\. Screening & Diagnostic Tools**

* **CRAFFT:** A validated screening tool designed specifically for pediatricians and professionals to identify risky substance use among adolescents 18, 19\.  
* **NPAS (Národní portál adiktologického screeningu):** A portal aggregating open-source screening tools for adults, including ASSIST (substance involvement), AUDIT (alcohol), DAST-10 (drug abuse), FTND (nicotine dependence), and SOGS (pathological gambling) 20-22.

**5\. Key Treatment Centers & Organizations**

* **SANANIM z.ú. – www.sananim.cz:** A major non-governmental organization operating outpatient clinics for youth and adults, therapeutic communities, day care, and online advisory services 23, 24\.  
* **Společnost Podané ruce o.p.s. – podaneruce.cz:** Provides comprehensive care centers, contact centers, outreach programs, and online counseling (e.g., EXTC) primarily in Moravia 25, 26\.  
* **Klinika Adiktologie (1. LF UK a VFN v Praze) – www.adiktologie.cz:** A premier academic and clinical institution that runs specialized outpatient clinics for children and youth, and develops evaluation tools for prevention programs 27-29.  
* **Child and Adolescent Detoxification Centre (CaADC):** Located at the Sisters of Mercy of St. Borromeo Hospital in Prague, specializing in inpatient detoxification and comprehensive therapy for patients under 18 30, 31\.  
* **Krajská adiktologická ambulance pro děti a dorost s.r.o. – www.kaadd.cz:** A specialized regional outpatient clinic for child and adolescent addictology located in Ostrava 32, 33\.

**6\. Academic Research & Publications**

* **Adiktologie v preventivní a léčebné praxi (Addictology in Prevention and Treatment Practice):** A professional journal publishing original research, case studies, and reviews, such as studies on outpatient clinics for youth 34, 35\.  
* **Je každé setkání lékaře s rodinou rodinná terapie?:** An article from *Psychiatrie pro praxi* emphasizing the role of systemic family therapy and narrative therapy in treating eating disorders and addictions 36, 37\.  
* **University Theses:** Academic evaluations of treatment effectiveness, including the bio-psycho-social-spiritual model in buprenorphine substitution treatment 38 and studies on the housing needs of clients graduating from residential aftercare centers 39\.
